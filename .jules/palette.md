## 2025-05-04 - Haptic Feedback and Screen Reader Actions
**Learning:** Combining haptic feedback with explicit accessibility actions for swipeable components significantly improves the perceived quality for all users. For screen reader users, custom actions (like 'togglePin') are much more discoverable than relying on gestures.
**Action:** Always include accessibilityActions and onAccessibilityAction when implementing swipe-to-action patterns in React Native.
